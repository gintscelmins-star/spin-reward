How it works

From game over to social media — fully automated

A fully automated process. Nothing to do manually — staff enters the results, the system handles the rest.

Staff enters the match results in the admin panel. The system automatically generates a personalized link for every player.

Players scan the QR code → see the leaderboard → find their name → open their personalized video card.
